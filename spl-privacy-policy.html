In one sentence: SPL Meter is a completely free, fully offline decibel meter app. We do not collect, store, upload, or share any of your data.

1. Overview

SPL Meter (the "App") is a tool that uses your device's microphone to measure ambient sound levels (sound pressure level) in real time. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information — in short, it doesn't.

2. We Collect No Data

The App:

  • Requires no internet connection and performs no network transmission
  • Does not use Firebase or any third-party analytics or tracking services
  • Contains no advertisements and no ad SDKs
  • Requires no account registration or sign-in
  • Collects and uploads no personal data or usage data of any kind

3. Microphone Permission

The App requests the Microphone permission solely to calculate ambient sound levels (decibels) in real time on your device. All audio processing happens locally and instantly:

  • Sound is never recorded or saved as audio files
  • Sound never leaves your device and is never uploaded to any server
  • The microphone stops being used when you close the App

4. Data Storage

If the App offers features such as measurement history or saved settings, that data is stored only in your device's local storage and can be deleted by you at any time. The developer has no access to this data.

5. Children's Privacy

Because the App collects no data whatsoever, it does not collect personal information from children and is suitable for users of all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy
