相機權限Camera permission

相機僅供放大顯示The camera is only for magnifying

為了作為放大鏡，App 需要存取您的相機以即時顯示放大後的影像。相機畫面只會即時顯示在螢幕上，不會被儲存、記錄或傳送到任何地方。除非您主動使用系統的螢幕截圖或拍照功能來保存畫面，否則 App 不會留存任何影像。您可以隨時在系統「設定」中撤銷相機權限。 To work as a magnifier, the app needs access to your camera to show a live, enlarged image. The camera feed is displayed on screen in real time and is never saved, recorded, or sent anywhere. The app keeps no images unless you choose to save one yourself using your device's own screenshot or photo feature. You can revoke camera access at any time in your system Settings.
